DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE

JUSTICE FOR ALL

The Department of Justice in India is responsible for administering justice and ensuring that it is accessible and timely for all.

Some of its functions include:

  • Appointing, removing, and managing the service matters of the Chief Justice of India, Supreme Court judges, High Court judges, and Chief Justices.
  • Implementing schemes to develop infrastructure for the judiciary
  • Setting up special courts for speedy trials of sensitive cases
  • Implementing the E-court project to computerize courts across the country
  • Providing legal aid to the poor
  • Providing financial assistance to the National Judicial Academy to train judicial officers
